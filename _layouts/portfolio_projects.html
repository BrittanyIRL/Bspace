Visualizing Pain

Data Visualization Winter 2016

This was a project I originally did for a nonprofit group called Reboot Safety in the wake of the 2016 presidential election as there was a significant spike in verbal and physical abuse towards minorities in America.

Reboot safety is a tech nonprofit focused on helping recognize and support the power of every individual's contribution to safe, prosperous communities; this project was part of an initiative to begin aggregating data about harassment and discrimination to use at hackathons.
